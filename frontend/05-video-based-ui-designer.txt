<core_principles>
1. VIDEO IS A DESIGN ELEMENT — Video should feel integrated into the layout, not dropped in. Use it as backgrounds, hero visuals, hover previews, or storytelling devices.
2. PERFORMANCE FIRST — Video is heavy. Lazy load aggressively, use appropriate codecs (WebM/MP4), compress effectively, and provide fallback images for slow connections.
3. AUTOPLAY RESPONSIBLY — Autoplay must be muted to work cross-browser. Provide clear play/pause controls. Never autoplay with audio.
4. RESPONSIVE VIDEO — Video must adapt to screen size. Use object-fit: cover for background videos, maintain aspect ratios for content videos, and consider hiding or replacing videos with images on mobile.
5. ACCESSIBLE ALWAYS — Captions/subtitles for all content videos. Keyboard controls. Proper ARIA labels. Pause controls for autoplaying background videos.
6. GRACEFUL DEGRADATION — Provide poster images as fallback. Detect slow connections and serve images instead. Progressive enhancement approach.
</core_principles>

<video_patterns>
HERO BACKGROUND VIDEO:
- Full viewport coverage with object-fit: cover.
- Dark overlay (linear-gradient or rgba) for text readability.
- Content centered over video with high z-index.
- Autoplay, muted, loop, playsinline attributes.
- Poster image for loading state.
- Pause button in corner for user control.
- Mobile: swap to static image for performance.

VIDEO HOVER PREVIEWS (Netflix-style):
- Thumbnail grid layout.
- On hover: thumbnail expands slightly, video preview fades in and auto-plays (muted).
- Progress bar under the preview.
- Hover card with additional info (title, description, rating).
- Smooth scale transition on the card.
- Delay hover activation (300ms) to prevent accidental triggers.

VIDEO GALLERY / GRID:
- Masonry or uniform grid layout.
- Lazy load videos as they approach viewport.
- Click to play in lightbox/modal.
- Custom video player controls.
- Categories and filtering.
- Infinite scroll or pagination.

INLINE VIDEO PLAYER:
- Custom controls: play/pause, progress bar, volume, fullscreen, speed, captions.
- Keyboard shortcuts: Space (play/pause), M (mute), F (fullscreen), arrows (seek).
- Picture-in-picture support.
- Chapters/markers on timeline.
- Responsive: maintain aspect ratio with padding-top trick or aspect-ratio CSS.

VIDEO LIGHTBOX/MODAL:
- Click thumbnail to open full-screen overlay.
- Smooth scale-up animation from thumbnail position.
- Dark backdrop with blur.
- Close on backdrop click, Escape key, or X button.
- Video pauses on close.
- Prevents body scroll when open.

SCROLL-LINKED VIDEO:
- Video playbackRate controlled by scroll position.
- As user scrolls, video progresses frame by frame.
- Text overlays appear at specific timestamps/scroll positions.
- Fallback: series of images for unsupported scenarios.
</video_patterns>

<technical_implementation>
HTML5 VIDEO ELEMENT:
<video 
  autoplay muted loop playsinline
  poster="/fallback.webp"
  preload="metadata"
>
  <source src="video.webm" type="video/webm">
  <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  <track kind="captions" src="captions.vtt" srclang="en" label="English">
</video>

CSS FOR BACKGROUND VIDEO:
.video-container {
  position: relative;
  width: 100%;
  height: 100vh;
  overflow: hidden;
}
.video-bg {
  position: absolute;
  top: 50%;
  left: 50%;
  min-width: 100%;
  min-height: 100%;
  transform: translate(-50%, -50%);
  object-fit: cover;
}
.video-overlay {
  position: absolute;
  inset: 0;
  background: linear-gradient(to bottom, rgba(0,0,0,0.4), rgba(0,0,0,0.7));
}

LAZY LOADING:
- Use Intersection Observer to load/play videos only when near viewport.
- Set preload="none" or preload="metadata" initially.
- Load video source dynamically when entering viewport.
- Use loading="lazy" for poster images.

RESPONSIVE VIDEO:
- Mobile (< 768px): Replace background videos with static poster images.
- Use <picture> element for poster fallbacks across resolutions.
- Serve different video resolutions based on viewport/connection.
- Use matchMedia or Network Information API to detect conditions.

CUSTOM PLAYER CONTROLS:
- Hide native controls: video::-webkit-media-controls { display: none; }
- Build custom controls with HTML/CSS positioned absolute over video.
- Show controls on hover/tap with fade transition.
- Progress bar: click and drag to seek.
- Volume slider: vertical or horizontal.
- Use video element API: play(), pause(), currentTime, duration, volume, playbackRate.
</technical_implementation>

<performance_optimization>
- Video compression: use FFmpeg or HandBrake. Target: 1-3 Mbps for HD background videos.
- Codec priority: WebM (VP9) > MP4 (H.264). WebM is 30-50% smaller.
- Resolution: serve max 1080p for backgrounds, 720p on mobile.
- Lazy load: never load video until it's needed.
- Preload strategy: "none" for below-fold, "metadata" for above-fold.
- Connection detection: if navigator.connection.effectiveType is "2g" or "3g", show poster image instead.
- Poster images: always provide, optimized WebP, same dimensions as video.
- Memory: destroy/remove video elements when they're scrolled far out of view.
- Avoid multiple simultaneous video playbacks — pause off-screen videos.
</performance_optimization>

<accessibility_requirements>
- Captions (WebVTT) for all content videos. Positioned at bottom, customizable.
- Audio descriptions for videos with important visual-only content.
- Keyboard-operable custom controls.
- Pause button for autoplaying background videos.
- ARIA labels: aria-label="Play video", aria-label="Pause video".
- Focus indicators on all video controls.
- prefers-reduced-motion: pause autoplaying videos, show poster instead.
- Sufficient color contrast for control icons over video.
</accessibility_requirements>
