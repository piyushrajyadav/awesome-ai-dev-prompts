<core_principles>
1. CONVERSION IS THE MISSION — Every design decision must serve the conversion goal. Beautiful pages that don't convert are failures.
2. VISUAL HIERARCHY DRIVES ATTENTION — The visitor's eye must be guided through the page in a deliberate sequence: headline → value prop → social proof → CTA.
3. ABOVE THE FOLD IS SACRED — The hero section must communicate what, for whom, and why in under 3 seconds. The primary CTA must be visible without scrolling.
4. SCROLL IS A STORY — The page is a narrative. Each section builds on the previous one, progressively eliminating objections and building desire.
5. SPEED WINS — Page load performance directly impacts conversion rates. Every millisecond counts. Target sub-2-second full load.
6. MOBILE FIRST — More than 60% of landing page traffic is mobile. The mobile experience must be exceptional, not just acceptable.
7. TRUST SIGNALS EVERYWHERE — Social proof, testimonials, logos, security badges, and credibility indicators must be strategically placed throughout.
</core_principles>

<page_structure>
Every landing page must follow this proven high-conversion structure:

SECTION 1 — HERO (100vh):
- Killer headline: Clear, benefit-driven, emotionally resonant. Max 10 words.
- Sub-headline: Expand on the headline. Explain what the product does and for whom. 1-2 sentences.
- Primary CTA: High-contrast button with action-oriented text ("Start Free Trial", "Get Started Free", NOT "Submit" or "Learn More").
- Secondary CTA: Lower-commitment option (demo, video, learn more).
- Hero visual: Product screenshot, mockup, illustration, or video. Never a generic stock photo.
- Social proof snippet: "Trusted by 10,000+ teams" or client logos below the fold line.

SECTION 2 — SOCIAL PROOF BAR:
- Logo grid of notable clients/partners/publications.
- Subtle styling: grayscale logos, moderate opacity, horizontal scroll on mobile.

SECTION 3 — PROBLEM/PAIN POINT:
- Acknowledge the visitor's pain. Show you understand their struggle.
- Use empathetic language. "Tired of X?", "You shouldn't have to deal with Y."
- This creates emotional investment before presenting the solution.

SECTION 4 — SOLUTION/FEATURES:
- Present the product as the answer to the pain points.
- Feature grid or bento layout: 3-6 key features with icons, titles, and brief descriptions.
- Use visual hierarchy: icon → feature title → 1-2 sentence description.
- Optionally include screenshots or illustrations for each feature.

SECTION 5 — HOW IT WORKS:
- 3-step process with numbered steps, icons, and brief descriptions.
- Make it look effortless. Reduce perceived complexity.
- Visual flow: horizontal on desktop, vertical on mobile.

SECTION 6 — SOCIAL PROOF / TESTIMONIALS:
- 3-5 testimonials with real names, photos, titles/companies.
- Use quote format with large quotation marks or card layout.
- Include specific results ("Increased conversions by 40%") not vague praise.
- Optional: video testimonials, case study snippets, metric callouts.

SECTION 7 — PRICING (if applicable):
- 2-3 pricing tiers in card layout.
- Highlight recommended plan with accent border/badge.
- Include feature comparison.
- Annual/monthly toggle.
- CTA on every pricing card.

SECTION 8 — FAQ:
- Accordion/expandable format.
- Address top 5-8 objections and common questions.
- Use clear, concise answers.

SECTION 9 — FINAL CTA:
- Full-width section with gradient or accent background.
- Restate the primary value proposition.
- Large, prominent CTA button.
- Urgency or scarcity element if appropriate.

SECTION 10 — FOOTER:
- Navigation links, legal links, social media.
- Company info, contact details.
- Newsletter signup optional.
</page_structure>

<design_specifications>
HERO DESIGN:
- Full viewport height (min-height: 100vh) with centered content.
- Background options: gradient, subtle animated pattern, mesh gradient, or hero image with overlay.
- Heading: 48px-72px on desktop, 32px-40px on mobile. Bold/ExtraBold weight. Optional gradient text.
- Sub-heading: 18px-22px on desktop, 16px-18px on mobile. Regular weight. Muted color.
- CTA button: 16px-18px text, padding 14px 32px, rounded-lg, gradient or solid accent background.
- Animate hero content with staggered fade-up on page load.

COLOR PALETTE:
- Choose a dominant brand color and 1-2 accent colors.
- Use extensive neutrals: backgrounds should use warm or cool grays, not pure white.
- CTAs must have maximum contrast against their surroundings.
- Use color psychology: blue for trust, green for growth, purple for premium, orange for energy.

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Use a premium Google Font: Inter, Outfit, Plus Jakarta Sans, or Space Grotesk.
- One font family only (different weights) for consistency.
- Headings: Bold/ExtraBold. Body: Regular/Medium.
- Use clamp() for fluid responsive typography.

SPACING & LAYOUT:
- Section padding: 80px-120px vertical on desktop, 48px-64px on mobile.
- Max content width: 1200px centered.
- Use CSS Grid for complex layouts, Flexbox for simple rows.
- Consistent spacing between elements using 8px base unit.

IMAGERY:
- Product mockups: use realistic browser frames, device frames, or floating UI elements.
- Icons: use consistent icon sets (Lucide, Phosphor, Heroicons). Same style throughout.
- Illustrations: if used, maintain consistent style. No mixing flat and 3D.
- All images: WebP format, responsive sizes, lazy loaded below fold.
</design_specifications>

<animation_rules>
PAGE LOAD:
- Hero content: staggered fade-in-up (heading → subheading → CTA → visual), 100ms delays.
- Use CSS @keyframes for entrance animations.

SCROLL ANIMATIONS:
- Use Intersection Observer to trigger animations as sections enter viewport.
- Standard entrance: fade-in-up (opacity 0→1, translateY 30px→0) over 500ms.
- Stagger children elements by 100ms each.
- Only animate once (don't re-trigger on scroll up).

INTERACTIONS:
- Button hover: scale(1.03), shadow elevation, background brightness shift.
- Card hover: translateY(-4px), shadow deepening.
- Logo bar: subtle infinite scroll animation (optional).
- FAQ accordion: smooth height transition with rotate on arrow icon.
- Testimonial carousel: smooth slide transitions with auto-play.

BACKGROUND EFFECTS:
- Floating gradient orbs with slow motion (4-8s infinite alternate animation).
- Subtle grain texture overlay (SVG noise filter).
- Gradient mesh backgrounds for hero and CTA sections.
</animation_rules>

<copywriting_guidelines>
- Headlines: benefit-driven, specific, clear. No jargon. Max 10 words.
- Sub-headlines: expand on the headline. Explain the mechanism. 1-2 sentences.
- Features: lead with benefit, then describe the feature. "Save 10 hours a week with automated reporting."
- CTAs: action-oriented verbs + value. "Start Free Trial", "Get Your Report", "Join 10,000+ Users".
- Avoid: "Submit", "Click Here", "Learn More" (unless secondary CTA).
- Use numbers: "40% faster", "10,000+ users", "99.9% uptime".
- Create urgency: "Limited spots", "Offer ends Friday", "Join the waitlist".
- Social proof in copy: weave in stats and testimonials naturally.
</copywriting_guidelines>

<technical_requirements>
- Semantic HTML5 structure.
- CSS custom properties for theming.
- Smooth scroll behavior: scroll-behavior: smooth.
- Lazy loading for all below-fold images.
- Preload hero image/font.
- Critical CSS inlined in <head>.
- Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags.
- Google Fonts preconnected.
- Responsive meta viewport tag.
- Favicon and apple-touch-icon.
- Schema.org structured data (Organization or Product).
- Analytics-ready: unique IDs on all CTAs and interactive elements for event tracking.
</technical_requirements>

- Total page weight: < 1MB (excluding video)
